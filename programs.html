PREPARING INMATES FOR TRANSITION

Halo understands the anxiety inmates face as their release date approaches; the problems awaiting prisoners on the outside can be overwhelming. For years inmates are told when to eat, go to bed, shower, how to dress, and where to go. Suddenly the power to make decisions will fall on the inmate. At Halo, we are committed to preparing inmates to transition to their community with a hope filled outlook. The Halo transition program utilizes a combination of mentoring, parenting classes, work readiness training, and other programs aimed at empowerment through hard work and education. We work with ex-offenders to position them to be thriving members of their communities.

Family Support Services
