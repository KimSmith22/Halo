PREPARING INMATES FOR TRANSITION

Halo understands the anxiety inmates face as their release date approaches; the problems awaiting prisoners on the outside can be overwhelming. For years inmates are told when to eat, go to bed, shower, how to dress, and where to go. Suddenly the power to make decisions will fall on the inmate. At Halo, we are committed to preparing inmates to transition to their community with a hope filled outlook. The Halo transition program utilizes a combination of mentoring, parenting classes, work readiness training, and other programs aimed at empowerment through hard work and education. We work with ex-offenders to position them to be thriving members of their communities. LEARN MORE

Ex-Offender Services

At Halo, we understand the difficulty ex-offenders face when employment seeking. Every day things most of us take for granted such as, personal grooming, proper attire, and interview skills are often overlooked when inmates transition back to their communities. LEARN MORE

Family Support Services

Halo's goal is to assist families with an incarcerated parent through empowerment programs aimed at strengthening the familial bond during the inmate's incarceration and after the transition from prison.LEARN MORE

Halo's Children Project

Through our community outreach, we have witnessed firsthand the strain on relationships between inmates and children caused by incarceration. Halo's Children Project aims to become the area's largest outreach program specifically for the children of inmates. LEARN MORE
